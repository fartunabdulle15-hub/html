Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela
1918–2013
South African anti-apartheid revolutionary, political leader, and philanthropist.
Quick Facts
  • Born: July 18, 1918 (Mvezo, South Africa)
  • Died: December 5, 2013
  • Political party: African National Congress (ANC)
  • Nobel Peace Prize: 1993
Timeline
  1. 1944 — Joined the African National Congress (ANC)
  2. 1962 — Imprisoned for anti-apartheid activities
  3. 1990 — Released from prison after 27 years
  4. 1993 — Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize
  5. 1994 — Elected as South Africa’s first Black president
Legacy
  • Symbol of peace and reconciliation
  • Helped end apartheid in South Africa
  • Inspired movements for freedom and justice worldwide
